NLP-driven emergency alert system for smartwatches-Graduation Project

September 2025

Implemented natural language processing to create a system that can smartly understand Arabic normal daily text and detect emergency distress calls based on context.
We built an Arabic emergency textual dataset with different and complex emergency and non-emergency sentences.
Due to difficulty in actually telling if someone is in danger in the right moment without making false alarms, we trained and integrated heart rate models to confirm a real emergency situations where person is in real danger.
